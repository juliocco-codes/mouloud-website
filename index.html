About Your Guide

Mouloud Tazint

I am a certified mountain guide based in Marrakech, and I have spent my life leading travellers through the landscapes of southern Morocco — the Sahara Desert, the Atlas Mountains, and the Atlantic coast.

I run a small, local travel operation built on a simple idea: fair trade tourism. We work with local families, buy from local markets, and travel with our own animals — mules in the mountains, camels in the desert. They are our companions, not rental equipment.

Our prices sit in the middle: not low-cost, not luxury. Good enough that you experience something real, fair enough that the money stays in the communities we pass through.

Whether you want to trek to the summit of Mount Toubkal, cross the Sahara by camel, or explore the kasbahs of the south by 4x4 — I will build a trip around what you want to see.

Our Tours

Travels & Packages

We organise holidays and travel from 2 days to 3 weeks. Every trip can start from any airport or city in Morocco. Choose your own combination of activities, or let us suggest a route.

Day Trips

Short escapes from Marrakech to Ait Benhaddou, the kasbahs of Ouarzazate, Ouzoud waterfalls, the desert camp at Zagora, or the coastal town of Essaouira. Perfect for a quick private trip when time is short.

2 days · From €220 per person

Sahara Safari

Your first real encounter with the Sahara. Travel by 4x4 to the dunes of Merzouga or Chegaga. Spend your nights in desert camps among the sand seas — one night in a fertile oasis valley, another under the stars in the open desert.

3 days · From €330 per person

Discovery Tours

A fully personalised journey through Morocco's wild interior. Choose to walk or ride. Move between valleys, mountain passes, and ancient medinas. Four days is enough to reach places most visitors never see.

3–4 days · From €440 per person

Mountain Trekking

Multi-day treks through the High Atlas with mules carrying your gear, or across the Sahara with camels. Professional local teams, all meals and camping included. Routes to any destination, including the summit of Mount Toubkal (4,167m).

5+ days · From €170 per person per day

Safari Tours

Choose your own route through southern Morocco by 4x4. Visit the destinations you want, spend as long as you like. Car, driver, accommodation and guide all included.

5+ days · From €150 per person per day

Week-Long Expeditions

Full-week tours departing from any airport or city in Morocco. Combine hiking, safari, and cultural visits into one extended journey. Special rates for longer trips.

6–7 days · From €555 per person

Custom & Thematic Trips

Longer stays, yoga retreats, meditation trips, special events, or simply a slow journey through Morocco at your own pace. Tell us what you want, and we will build it. The longer you stay, the deeper you go.
